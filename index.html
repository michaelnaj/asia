Asia in the South: Five Individual Narratives of Duke Students

by Michael Najjar

This website is dedicated to my friends and their experiences as Asians in the South of the United States of America. All of the stories detail different perspectives and are intended to present a well-rounded view of the position of Asian students in the Duke community. Each video is shot close-up and portrait in order to emphasise the personal nature of these tales and the inherent meaning they hold to the people telling them. Each person volunteered because they felt that they had something to say that needed to be heard. Some are small stories of single instances, while others are longer winding narratives. Every story here carries importance, be it short or long.
